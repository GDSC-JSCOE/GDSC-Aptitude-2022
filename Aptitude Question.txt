11. With the money I have, I can buy 50 pens or 150 pencils. I kept 10% aside for taxi fare. With the remaining, I purchased 54 pencils and P pens. What is the value of P?
1) 32
2) 30
3) 27
4) None  of  these  

Solution:
Since cost of, 50 pens = 150 pencils, With the cost of 3 pencils I can buy 1 pen.
After putting aside 10% for taxi I was left with 90% of the money, with which I can buy 135 pencils (90% of 150) or 45(90% of 50) pens,
I bought 54 pencils and P pens, or I could have bought (54 + 3P) pencil,
54 + 3P = 135,
3P = 135 - 54 = 81,
P = 27

Answer : 3) 27

12. Ram sells an article at three-fourth of its list price and makes a loss of 10%. Find the profit percentage if he sells at the list price.
1) 20%
2) 25%
3) 15%
4) None of these

Solution:
Let the list price be x,
      S.P = 3/4x, S.P = (100 – loss%) / 100*C.P = 0.9 C.P
       3/4x = 0.9 C.P, C.P = 3x / 3.6
       If S.P = x, Profit %  = (x- 3x/3.6 ) / ( 3x/3.6 ) * 100 = 60/3 = 20%
 
 Answer : 1) 20%
