the total sum of their investment is Rs. 17880, how much amount did Raghu
invested?
1) Rs. 5000
2) Rs. 6500
3) Rs. 6000
4) Rs. 7500
5) Rs. 5500
Ans RS.6000

Solution:
Let the investment by Raghu be x
Mohit = x * 90/100 = 9x/10
Pradeep = 9x/10 * 120/100 = 108x/100
x + 9x/10 + 108x/100 = 17880
x = Rs. 6000
